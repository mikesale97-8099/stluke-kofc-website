Who Can Join?

Basic requirements for membership

‚úì Membership Requirements

The Knights of Columbus is open to men who meet the following criteria:

  • Catholic in good standing - A practicing Catholic who attends Mass regularly and receives the sacraments
  • At least 18 years of age - There are no upper age limits
  • Family-oriented - Whether married or single, committed to supporting Catholic family values
  • Willing to serve - Ready to contribute time and talent to charitable works in your community

That's it! If you meet these basic requirements and want to grow in faith while serving others, we'd love to have you join our council.

How to Join

The path to becoming a Knight is simple

1

Reach Out to Us

Send us an email at [email protected] expressing your interest in joining. You can also attend one of our monthly meetings (First Thursday at 7:00 PM) or talk to any Knight after Mass.

2

Meet with a Knight

One of our members will reach out to answer your questions, explain what membership involves, and help you complete a simple membership application. There's no pressure‚Äîjust a friendly conversation about the Knights.

3

Attend Your First Degree Ceremony

The First Degree ceremony is a meaningful ritual that formally welcomes you into the Order. It's a spiritual experience that emphasizes our commitment to charity and explains the principles we hold dear. Family members are often invited to attend part of the ceremony.

4

Get Involved

Start participating in council activities at your own pace. Attend meetings, volunteer at events, and get to know your brother Knights. You'll quickly discover opportunities to serve that match your interests and availability.

Common Questions

Answers to what prospective members often ask

How much time is required?

As much or as little as you can give! Some Knights attend monthly meetings and help at a few events per year. Others are involved weekly. You choose your level of involvement based on your schedule and interests.

Are there membership dues?

Yes, there are modest annual dues that help cover council operating expenses. Contact us for current rates. Dues help fund our charitable work and keep the council running.

Can my family be involved?

Absolutely! While the Knights is a men's organization, many of our events are family-friendly and we actively encourage family participation. Wives and children are welcome at many activities throughout the year.

What if I'm new to the parish?

Perfect! Joining the Knights is a wonderful way to get connected at St. Luke. You'll quickly meet other men in the parish and find your place in the community. We welcome new parishioners warmly.
