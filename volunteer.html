Blood Drive
3 times per year
Help organize blood drives and save lives through donations to the Indiana Blood Bank.

Ways to Help:

  • Coordinate sign-ups after Mass
  • Donate blood
  • Assist donors
  • Serve refreshments

3
Fall Festival - Grillin' Knights
Annual - September
Man the food booth at St. Luke's annual Fall Festival. Grill, serve, and be a visible presence for the Knights!

Ways to Help:

  • Set up and take down
  • Kitchen prep
  • Serve food
  • Grill burgers and brats
Sign Me Up
4
Youth Soccer Challenge
Annual - September
Help young athletes test their soccer skills in this fun competition.

Ways to Help:

  • Sign up youth participants
  • Set up field and equipment
  • Run the competition
  • Keep score and encourage kids
Sign Me Up
5
40 Days for Life
2 times per year
Peaceful prayer vigil for the dignity of human life at 86th & Georgetown.

Ways to Help:

  • Coordinate sign-ups at Mass
  • Pray for 1 hour at vigil site
  • Witness for life
Sign Me Up
6
Pancake Breakfast
3 times per year
Our famous all-you-can-eat pancake breakfasts raise funds for charitable programs.

Ways to Help:

  • Set up and take down
  • Cook pancakes
  • Serve food
  • Pour coffee and clean tables
Sign Me Up
7
Coats for Kids
Annual - Late October
Provide warm winter coats to students at Holy Angels School. Make sure every child stays warm!

Ways to Help:

  • Purchase coordination
  • Distribute coats at school
  • Fit coats for children

Youth Basketball Free Throw

Ways to Help:

  • Keep score
Sign Me Up

Baby Bottle Drive

  • Advertise the campaign
