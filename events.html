Meetings & Events

Join us for fellowship, service, and community

Regular Council Meetings

When: First Thursday of each month at 7:00 PM
Where: Blessed Fr. Michael McGivney Room
St. Luke Catholic Church
7575 Holliday Drive East, Indianapolis, IN
What: Business meeting followed by fellowship and refreshments. All members are encouraged to attend.

New members and prospective members are always welcome to attend and see what we're all about!

Basketball Free Throw Contest

Annual youth basketball free throw competition. Open to all parish youth. Test your skills and win prizes!

Baby Bottle Campaign

Pro-Life fundraiser in partnership with the St. Luke Pro-Life Committee. Baby bottles available to fill with donations for local pregnancy support centers.

Special Knights of Columbus Mass at 11:30 AM. All knights, families, and prospective members invited to attend.

Beginning of the Lenten season. Join us in our Lenten observances and charitable activities.

Fish Fry #1

Our famous Lenten fish fry! Delicious fried and baked fish dinners. Dine-in and carry-out available. Proceeds support our charitable programs.

Second Lenten fish fry dinner. Come enjoy great food and fellowship while supporting our charitable work in the community.

Annual food drive benefiting the St. Vincent de Paul Boulevard Place Food Pantry. Our goal: 10,000 lbs of food! Donation bins available at the parish.
